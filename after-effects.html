LILI's FILM #2 - LISA Dance Performannce Video Edited in After Effects with motion typography and transitions.

About This Project

This After Effects rotoscope animation is inspired by LILI’s FILM #2 featuring Lisa of BLACKPINK. It involves frame-by-frame masking, motion tracking, and animated typography synced to dance movements. This project explores kinetic rhythm and highlights smooth transitions using graph editors and blend modes.
