How Old Are You on Other Planets?

Mercury

—

Mercury has the shortest year — only 88 Earth days. You’d celebrate more birthdays here!

Venus

—

A year on Venus is about 225 Earth days. Still faster than Earth, but not too quick!

Earth

—

Well... you're already used to this one. 365 days = 1 year.

Mars

—

Mars takes almost 2 Earth years to orbit the Sun. That means half as many birthdays.

Jupiter

—

A year on Jupiter is nearly 12 Earth years long. Your age drops big time here!

Saturn

—

Saturn's year lasts 29.5 Earth years. You're still a child here!

Uranus

—

Uranus takes 84 Earth years to orbit the Sun. One birthday = a lifetime.

Neptune

—

Neptune's year lasts 165 Earth years. You'd barely turn one!

Pluto

—

Pluto takes almost 250 Earth years to go around the Sun. Eternal youth!
